# Curated Crops
A Minecraft mod that adds a curated selection of crops to the game, focusing on quality over quantity for implementation and balance.

## Berries
Berries like the vanilla Minecraft berries, also grow on bushes.
- Blueberries: Found in old growth pine taiga and taiga biomes.
- Raspberries: Found in meadows, windswept forests, and windswept hill biomes.
- Strawberries: Found in flower forests, meadows, and plains biomes.

## Crops
Crops that grow in tilled soil, like vanilla Minecraft crops.
- Corn: Found in plains, savannas, and sunflower plains biomes.

## Foods
Foods are crafted from other ingredients such as berries, crops, and other vanilla items.
- Cheese
- Roasted Corn
- Taco
- Tortilla

## Inspirations

- [Croptopia](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/croptopia)

- [Natura](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/natura)
